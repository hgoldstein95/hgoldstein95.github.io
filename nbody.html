n-Body Simulation

  • Worked with a partner to create a Python simulation of the "n-body problem", a common problem in physics that can be modeled as n planets in gravitational orbit.
  • Used the classical Runge-Kutta method to calculate planet positions over time, and vPython to generate a 3D graphical representation.
  • Project was completed in the Science and Engineering Learning Center at Manalapan High School.
